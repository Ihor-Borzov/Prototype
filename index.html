The floor you enjoy with no compromises

Eco friendly

Vinyl is a popular flooring material for homes with kids because it's unaffected by water, resistant to stains, easy to take care of, inexpensive, and very durable. It's very difficult to rip, tear, or damage vinyl flooring. In most cases, vinyl flooring can stand up to daily life with children. floor manufacturers have eliminated the use of chemical and now provide 'phthalates-free' vinyl flooring.

Highly durable

Vinyl flooring is highly durable. If installed and maintained correctly, it can last upwards of 10-20 years. That said, vinyl is a great choice for any room in your house that get the most foot traffic. It is durable enough to be placet even in the gym. It also allows moisture to evaporate quickly, making it a good option for hot yoga studios. Additionally, most vinyl flooring has a wear layer on its surface that resists scratches and stains.
